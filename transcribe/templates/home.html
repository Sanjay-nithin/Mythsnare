🧭 Why We Took On This Project

In today’s hyper-connected world, misinformation is no longer just an inconvenience — it’s a global threat. Whether it's about health, elections, education, or public policy, fake content can go viral in seconds, shaping public opinion before facts even surface.

We built MisinfoDetector because we believe truth shouldn’t be buried under noise. Our goal is to empower users with smart tools that help evaluate information accurately, across both voice and text inputs.

📌 OUR EXPERIENCE: COVID-19 Vaccine Rumor in India

A viral WhatsApp message falsely claimed COVID vaccines caused infertility. The misinformation delayed immunizations, triggered fear, and caused real harm. MisinfoDetector aims to prevent such consequences with instant analysis and verification.

These events reinforce the urgent need for tools that detect, contextualize, and counter misinformation instantly.
